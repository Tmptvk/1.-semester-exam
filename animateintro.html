Adobe Animate

The first time we were introduced to animate, we were asked to make a simple animation of a car driving.
I had never tried the program before but was very excited to see what it could offer. I quickly learned
that using this program, would perhaps not be as easy as I thought. We started the assignment in class
so we had time to ask our teacher as well as our fellow students for help. At home we were then supposed
to finish our animation, and upload it so everyone could see it. Before we started animating the car
driving, we were asked to make a storyboard, to sketch out our idea. Both the storyboard and the
animation itself can be seen below.
