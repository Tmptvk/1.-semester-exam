Stop motion movie

As the intro project on the Multimediadesign education, everyone on the 1st semester was asked to make a stop motion movie.
The day before the project began, we had all been given 2 sticky notes, one green and one purple. On one of them, we were asked
to write something we wished to do in our life, and on the other one we wrote about something we disliked. When the project began
we were put into randomly decided groups, and had to pick one project leader. The project leader then had to go and pick 2 random
sticky notes, from all of the ones written the day before. Whatever was written on these sticky notes, was then our subject for the
stop motion movie. In our group, we ended up with the subjects “I want to be remembered for something” and “I hate to regret”

If you don’t know what a stop motion movie is, then think of it as a series of pictures that moves fast to give the illusion of movement.
A couple of examples of well known stop motion movies could be “Wallace and Gromit” or the newer “Kubo and the Two Strings”
