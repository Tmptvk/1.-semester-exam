Project 1

This project was our first. We were randomly split into groups in our class
and was then given one out of 2 website. My group got the website “http://www.fyensrideklub.dk/”
The project was about redesigning the website, which we had been given. This means
we had to discover what problems the website has, and then figure out a way to solve them.
The first thing we did in our group, was fill out a NUG test, to see what each of us
thought of the website, and how bad or good some of the things were.
After we had done that, we all agreed to start doing as much research as possible.
We researched what riding actually was, who used those clubs and what it meant to them.
We wanted to get a deeper understanding of the kind of people who we had to design this website to.
When we first started our redesign, we started of by making a logo for the website.
This was because there was no visible logo anywhere on the page, and we felt like it
would be a good idea to have, so users can more easily identity the page. See the logo here


After the logo was done, we then started designing the actual home page. We settled for some
colors and what information was actually important enough to show as the landing page.
All of the redesigning was done in the program "Adobe XD"
