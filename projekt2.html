Project 2

This project was our 2nd big assignment, and it was done in collaboration with the
H.C. Andersen museum. The assignment was to make an interactive installation for the museum.
It had to be enticing and have some kind of replay ability, to keep customers interested and
make them want more It had to be an installation that everyone would find enjoyable, as the
museum has people coming from all ages and backgrounds. In this project, we were
allowed to pick our own groups, which made it more enjoyable to work on.

To begin with, our group came up with a bunch of different ideas, to get as much on the
table as possible. Slowly we then started to narrow our choices down, until we were
left with 1. Our idea was to make a “chose your own adventure” style of installation.

In the project, we also made a couple of posters, to back up our presentation of the final product
one of which you can see here below

The concept was to go on an adventure, as H.C. Andersen trough Europe as he did, just
with more choices to make it have replay value. All of our destinations and choices came
from a book written by H.C. Andersen himself, so everything you can do is realistic.
The idea is to get into a train coupe, and then have a screen in the middle of the room
with a bench on either side.
